# Code of Ethical and Business Conduct

Texagon aims to create and maintain an environment that is ethical and respectful towards all employees and business operations. We expect all organization members to comply with the rules and regulations and take responsibility for their actions. This Code of Ethical and Business Conduct applies to all stakeholders and members of the company regardless of gender, age, language, caste, ethnicity, religion, cultural background, nationality, beliefs, disabilities, or any other form of physical or mental difference.

This code is in place to help establish an acceptable and positive working environment for all organization members. All employees are responsible for upholding this code by ensuring that any observed injustice or unfair practice is reported immediately to the relevant authorities (management, human resources personnel, or any direct supervisor). This helps the company maintain a healthy and positive environment by involving all employees in the process.

Employees are encouraged to report any information regarding violations of this code without fear of reprisal or retaliation. Employees who wish to remain anonymous can report such matters to HR confidentially. Any failure to comply with this code of conduct may lead to disciplinary actions, up to and including immediate termination of employment.

## Code of Conduct

1. **Ethical and Respectful Behavior:**\
   All employees are required to act and behave ethically and respectfully towards all other employees, customers, and company staff at all times. This includes maintaining their actions and words within respectable boundaries. Employees are expected to use kind words, maintain professional boundaries with colleagues, and show everyone equal respect.

2. **Commitment to Company Interests:**\
   Texagon’s business ventures demand that employees act in the company’s best interest, both ethically and legally.

3. **Non-Disruption Clause:**\
   Employees must agree that during and after their employment with Texagon, they will not attempt to disrupt, damage, or slander the company or its relationships with customers, members, employees, or external parties.

4. **Dedication to Employment:**\
   Employees are required to give their complete commitment to Texagon during their employment. They may not engage in any activity that disrupts their work commitment or leads to non-compliance with this code of conduct.

5. **Protection of Company Property:**\
   Employees must not engage in any act that damages the company’s tangible or intangible property.

6. **Punctuality and Timeliness:**\
   Texagon values punctuality and expects employees to strictly adhere to prescribed work timings. Adjustments may occur based on business demands, such as during public or company events.

7. **Harassment and Discrimination Prohibited:**\
   Any form of harassment or discrimination based on sex, age, caste, ethnic background, religious beliefs, sect, disability, medical condition, marital status, caregiving responsibilities, or other factors is strictly prohibited. This includes physical and verbal harassment in any form. Employees are encouraged to foster an inclusive environment where people from different backgrounds are treated equally and with respect.

8. **Unacceptable Actions:**\
   While it is impossible to list every undesirable action, employees must use sensible judgment to avoid behaviors such as falsification, lying, defamation, omission of facts, disclosure of trade secrets, threatening others, verbal or physical abuse, long-term patterns of absenteeism or tardiness, possession of illegal items, and unauthorized access to documents or property.

## Reporting and Enforcement

1. All employees must report observed violations of this code to the appropriate authority (management, HR, or direct supervisors).
2. Employees reporting violations will be protected from retaliation and may report anonymously if they prefer.
3. Violations of this code will be subject to strict investigation and disciplinary actions, up to and including termination of employment.

## Conclusion

Employment at Texagon is a result of mutual consent and should be taken seriously. Defiance or disregard of this code will result in appropriate disciplinary measures. Employees are expected to uphold this code to foster a respectful, ethical, and positive work environment for all.

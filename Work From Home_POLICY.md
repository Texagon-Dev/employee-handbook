# Work From Home (WFH) Policy

## 1. Eligibility
- **Not Eligible:** HR, Sales, and Marketing teams are not allowed to work from home.  
- **Eligible (Conditional):** Development team may request WFH **only for genuine reasons**, and proof can be asked at any time.  

## 2. Request Process
- Employees must email their WFH request to their **reporting manager** and copy the **HR Department at hr@texagon.io**.  
- Requests should be submitted in advance (except in emergencies).  
- Approval is subject to management discretion.  

## 3. Expectations During WFH
- Remain available during official working hours.  
- Be responsive on official communication channels.  
- Deliver tasks on time.  

## 4. Non-Compliance
- Submitting false claims or misusing this policy will result in:  
  - Rejection of future WFH requests, and/or  
  - Disciplinary action as per company policy.

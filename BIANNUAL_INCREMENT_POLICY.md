#  Texagon Biannual Increment Policy

At Texagon, we believe in recognizing and rewarding consistent performance. To support this, we offer **biannual (every 6 months) salary increments**, based entirely on individual performance.

## Performance-Based Increments

- Every team member’s performance will be **evaluated twice a year**.
- The evaluation is based on work quality, collaboration, initiative, Behavioure, and reliability.

## Important Note:

 **Increments are not automatic.**  
 If your performance during the evaluation period does not meet expectations, you **may not receive an increment** for that cycle.

This ensures that growth and rewards are aligned with contribution and effort.

If you have questions about your performance or goals, feel free to talk with your manager. We're here to help you grow and succeed!

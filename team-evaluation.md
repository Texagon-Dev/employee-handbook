## Purpose

Texagon is committed to continuous improvement and professional growth. The Team Evaluation Policy ensures that every project concludes with a structured assessment of team performance, fostering a culture of learning and excellence.

## Scope

This policy applies to all project teams at Texagon. Evaluations are conducted by the **Team Lead** and **Project Manager** upon project completion.

## Evaluation Process

1. **Timing**: Evaluations occur at the end of each project before final closure.
2. **Participants**: The **Team Lead** and **Project Manager** separately assess team performance.
3. **Method**: A standardized evaluation document is completed for each team.

## Evaluation Criteria

The evaluation document will include the following key sections:

### 1. Strengths

- Identify key skills and competencies demonstrated by the team.
- Highlight instances of effective collaboration, problem-solving, and technical excellence.
- Acknowledge outstanding contributions by individuals and the team as a whole.

### 2. Areas for Improvement

- Identify gaps in skills, knowledge, or processes that affected the project.
- Highlight specific challenges that impacted performance and suggest ways to overcome them.
- Address any recurring issues that require attention for future projects.

### 3. Suggestions for Growth

- Recommend training, workshops, or mentorship programs to improve skills.
- Suggest process improvements for better efficiency and productivity.
- Encourage knowledge-sharing initiatives within the team.

### 4. General Notes

- Capture additional observations not covered in the above sections.
- Include feedback on project-specific challenges, client interactions, or technical hurdles.
- Provide any relevant insights that could benefit future projects.

## Review & Action Plan

- The completed evaluation document is reviewed by senior management.
- Actionable insights are used to refine training programs and project workflows.
- Teams are encouraged to discuss feedback in a constructive manner.

## Impact on Employee Growth

- The evaluation results will be used to determine increments, bonuses, promotions, and long-term career growth within the company.
- Performance assessments will influence an employee's future at Texagon, ensuring fair recognition and reward for contributions.
- Constructive feedback will help employees align with the company’s expectations and professional development goals.

## Confidentiality & Transparency

- Evaluation documents are confidential and accessible only to relevant stakeholders.
- Feedback should be objective, constructive, and aimed at team growth.

---

Texagon is committed to fostering a positive and productive work environment through structured evaluations that drive continuous improvement. This policy ensures that every project contributes to the professional development of our teams and the overall success of our company.

# Frappe HR Documentation for Texagon ERP

Welcome to the Frappe HR documentation for Texagon ERP. This guide will help employees navigate the HR module within the Texagon ERP system (accessible at [erp.texagon.io](https://erp.texagon.io)).

## **1. Logging into the System**

1. Open your browser and navigate to [erp.texagon.io](https://erp.texagon.io).
2. Enter your login credentials (Username and Password).
3. Click **Login** to access the dashboard.

## **2. Checking In and Checking Out**

Employees must check in and check out using the ERP system, as this will be responsible for recording your attendance:

1. Go to **HR > Shift & Attendance > Employee Checkin**.
2. Click **Add Employee Checkin**
3. Change Logtype to **Check-in** when you start your work.
4. Change Logtype to **Check-out** when you finish your work.
5. Click **Fetch Geolocation** and click _Save_
6. Your attendance record will be updated automatically after a while.

## **3. Creating a Leave Request**

To apply for leave:

1. Navigate to **HR > Leaves > Leaves Application**.
2. Click on **Add Leave Application**.
3. Select the leave type (e.g., Casual Leave, Sick Leave, Leave without Pay).
4. Choose the start and end date of your leave.
5. Add any relevant notes (optional).
6. Click **Submit** for approval.

Once submitted, the leave request will be sent to your manager for approval.

## **4. Viewing Attendance Records**

Employees can review their attendance records by:

1. Going to **HR > Shift & Attendance > Attendance**.
2. Searching for their attendance records by entering their name or employee ID.
3. Viewing their attendance status (Present, Absent, Half-day, or Work-from-home).

## **5. Viewing Payslips**

To view and download payslips:

1. Navigate to **Payroll > Salary Slip**.
2. Locate the payslip for the desired month.
3. Click **View** to see the breakdown of salary, deductions, and net pay.
4. Click **Download** to save a copy of your payslip.

## **6. User Profile vs. Employee Profile**

- **User Profile**: This is linked to system access and permissions. It includes login credentials, email ID, and role-based access controls. **Users**
- **Employee Profile**: This contains personal and job-related details, such as designation, department, salary, and contact information. **HR > Employee**

## **7. Updating Employee Profile Information**

To update your employee profile:

1. Navigate to **HR > Employee**.
2. Click on your profile.
3. Update necessary details, including:
   - Personal Information (Name, Date of Birth, Contact Details)
   - Bank Details (For Salary Processing)
   - Emergency Contact Information
4. Click **Save** to update your information.

For any issues or assistance, please contact your HR administrator at Texagon.

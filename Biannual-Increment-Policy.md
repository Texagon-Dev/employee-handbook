At Texagon, we believe in recognizing and rewarding consistent performance. To support this, we offer **biannual (every 6 months) salary increments**, based entirely on individual performance.

## Performance-Based Increments

- **Performance is evaluated twice a year** in **June** and **December**.
- Evaluations are based on key criteria including:
  - Work quality  
  - Collaboration  
  - Initiative  
  - Behavior  
  - Reliability
- **The evaluation period begins after successful completion of the probation or internship period.**
- Increments are **not automatic**. If performance during the evaluation period does not meet expectations, no increment may be granted.

## Increment Cycle Guidelines

- If your 6-month tenure completes **before or after** an evaluation month (June or December), your performance will be considered in the **next scheduled evaluation cycle**.  
  - For example: If your 6-month mark is in **July**, your performance will be reviewed in **December**.
- Upon successful evaluation:
  - You will receive an **increment letter**.
  - Your **new salary** will be applicable from the **1st of the following month** (i.e., **1st July** for June evaluation or **1st January** for December evaluation).
 
  

- **Note:** Management reserves the right to withhold or cancel performance evaluations and related increments due to specific circumstances or internal decisions.

# Leaves Policy

## Leave Management

We trust our team to act responsibly and be accountable for their actions. Leaves can be taken as needed, but they must be pre-planned, structured, and approved by the manager in advance, except in cases of emergencies.

- **Paid Leaves**:

  - Each full-time employee is entitled to **5 paid casual leaves** and **8 paid sick leaves** per calendar year.
  - The calendar year for all leave categories runs from January to December.
  - **Casual leaves** are accrued on a pro-rata basis, at **0.42 days per month** (calculated as 5 days divided by 12 months).
  - **Sick leaves** are accrued on a pro-rata basis, at **0.67 days per month** (calculated as 8 days divided by 12 months).
  - Leave entitlement is calculated based on the month and date of employment start within the given year.
  - **Leaves do not carry over** to the next year.

- **Leave Request Procedure**:

  - Every leave must be requested **at least one week in advance**; otherwise, it will not be approved.
  - Leave requests made just one or two days prior will not be accepted, except in emergencies.
  - Each team member must obtain approval from their reporting head, project manager, HR, and other relevant team or project members.
  - Leave requests should be submitted **via email** and approved by the designated individuals in the proper format.

- **Accrued Leaves**:

  - Leaves can only be taken from the **earned balance** at any given time, as they are accrued monthly.
  - If the requested leaves exceed the earned balance, approval will depend on project and team needs, as well as management's discretion. Extra leaves may be taken as **unpaid**, depending on the business situation.

- **Casual Leave Approval**:
  - Approval must be obtained from the relevant supervisor or project manager before HR can give final approval.
  - Taking leave in advance from next month's allotment is at HR's discretion, based on individual performance and the current business situation.

---

### a. Casual Leaves

Each team member is entitled to **5 paid casual leaves** per calendar year, to be used for emergencies or unforeseen circumstances.

- Casual leaves are accrued on a pro-rata basis, meaning **0.42 days per month** (calculated as 5 days divided by 12 months).
- Casual leave requests must be submitted **via email** with **at least one full day’s notice** or before the workday begins.
- For leave longer than one day, requests should be made **at least two weeks in advance**.
- Proof of the emergency or circumstance is required to approve leave.
- Approval is subject to the team’s needs and management’s discretion.
- Leave requests made within a shorter notice period may not be approved unless deemed necessary by the manager or HR.

---

### b. Sick Leaves

Each team member is entitled to **8 paid sick leaves** per calendar year.

- Sick leaves are accrued on a pro-rata basis, meaning **0.67 days per month** (calculated as 8 days divided by 12 months).
- Sick leave requests must be submitted **via email** at least one full day before the leave or by **08:00 AM on the day of absence**.
- If three or more sick days are requested, a **doctor’s note** is required.
- Failure to provide necessary documentation may result in **unpaid leave** or denial of the request.
- All relevant team members, HR, and the team lead/supervisors must be informed of the situation.
- Failure to follow this procedure may result in **disciplinary actions**, including deductions from leave balances, unpaid leave, or, in extreme cases, termination of employment due to repeated violations of the leave policy.

---

### c. Unpaid Leaves

- **Unpaid leaves** are allowed if business operations are not affected. These leaves require **prior approval** from the management.
- Employers reserve the right to reject leave applications if absence would negatively impact business operations.

---

- **Leave Request Procedure**:

  - Every leave must be requested **at least one week in advance**; otherwise, it will not be approved.
  - Leave requests made just one or two days prior will not be accepted, except in emergencies.
  - Each team member must:
    - **Email** their **HR** and **reporting manager** with the leave details.
    - **Create a leave application** on the **ERP system**.
    - Ensure that **manager approval is mandatory** for the leave to be processed.
  - Leave will only be considered approved once the manager has approved it on ERP or confirmed it via email.


### Note

These guidelines, policies, and procedures are designed to ensure consistency and fairness in all processes, ensuring that every active team member is treated equally in accordance with Texagon’s established policies. At Texagon, we are committed to maintaining a workplace free from discrimination, while implementing an efficient workflow system.

If any team member has a specific or unique query, they are encouraged to reach out to the HR representative for assistance.

This document has been approved and published by Texagon’s management and may be updated or modified as needed, at the discretion of the management.

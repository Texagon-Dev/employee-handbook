## Purpose

This policy establishes clear working hours and ensures adequate provisions for lunch and prayer breaks during office hours to accommodate employees’ needs.

**Policy Effective Date:** 13th Oct 2025
# Office Timing Policy
---

## Winter Schedule  
**Effective From:** October 2025  
**Until:** February 2026  

- **Monday to Thursday:** 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM  
- **Friday:** 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM  

---

## Summer Schedule  
**Effective From:** 1st March 2026  
**Until:** October 2026  

- **Monday to Thursday:** 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM  
- **Friday:** 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM  


---

## Break Schedule

To ensure productivity and accommodate personal needs, the following breaks will be observed:

### Lunch Break

- **Duration:** 01 Hour
- **Timing:** 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM

### **Friday Break**

- **Duration:** 1.5 hours
- **Timing:** 1:00 PM to 2:30 PM

---

## Break Guidelines

1. **Adherence:** Employees are encouraged to adhere strictly to the allocated times for each break.
2. **Prayer Breaks:** Designed to provide sufficient time for employees to perform prayers without interrupting workflow.
3. **Adjustments:** The exact timing of prayer breaks may be adjusted during the year to align with varying prayer times.
4. **Supervisors’ Role:** Supervisors are responsible for ensuring that the break schedule does not affect business operations.

---

## Implementation

- Employees should coordinate with their teams to ensure proper coverage during prayer and lunch breaks.
- Managers are encouraged to support employees in utilizing these breaks appropriately.

---

## Flexibility

- **Special Periods:** Flexibility in break timings may be provided during specific periods (e.g., Ramadan) to accommodate longer prayer times or fasting needs.
- **Adjustments:** Managers can make changes to the break schedule and must communicate these adjustments in advance to employees.

---

## Compliance

- All employees are expected to comply with the break timings to maintain a balance between personal and professional responsibilities.
- Managers must ensure the policy is implemented fairly and equitably across all teams.
- Managers, Leads, PMs, and the COO have the discretion to modify break timings as necessary.

---

## Additional Notes

- **Prayer Time Alignment:** Adjustments to prayer break timings will be made throughout the year to align with the exact prayer times.
- **Queries:** If any team member has specific or unique queries, they are encouraged to reach out to the HR representative for assistance.
